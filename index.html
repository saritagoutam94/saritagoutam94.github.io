WELCOME TO MPS PUNE

MPS Pune is one of the most trusted name in quality education, which is recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence. Maharashtra Public School Pune was the second initiative of Takshila Educational Society. It was established in 2003 and is a collaborative effort with the Maharashtra Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. MPS Pune is affiliated to CBSE, New Maharashtra and offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII.

In a very short span of time MPS Pune has established itself as one of the best K-12 institutions in western India. MPS has proliferated into a multidisciplinary institution, offering various facilities to impart varied expertise to keep pace with the world .The school offers courses that focus on holistic development of individuals by cultivating their unique talents.
